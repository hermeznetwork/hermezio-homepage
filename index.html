Hermez

Scalable token transfers on the wings of Ethereum.

Hermez is a zk-rollup focused on scaling payments and token transfers on top of Ethereum. It can handle up to 2000 transactions-per-second, and is designed with high-frequency tokens like ETH, DAI, and wBTC in mind.
